The Birthstone Collection

Discover the beauty, history, and symbolism behind each month's unique gemstone. Find the piece that resonates with your soul.

January

Garnet

Symbolizing trust and friendship, the Garnet is believed to protect the wearer during their travels.

February

Amethyst

A stone of clarity and calm, the Amethyst brings peace to the mind and strength to the spirit.

March

Aquamarine

Evoking the purity of crystalline waters, this stone represents hope, health, and youth.

April

Diamond

The ultimate symbol of eternal love and invincibility. A timeless choice for those born in April.

May

Emerald

A symbol of rebirth and love. The vivid green Emerald is believed to grant the wearer foresight and good fortune.

June

Pearl

Pearls represent purity and integrity. They are the only gemstones created by a living creature, making them truly unique.

July

Ruby

The "King of Gems," the Ruby represents passion, courage, and prosperity with its fiery red brilliance.

August

Peridot

Known as the "Evening Emerald," Peridot symbolizes strength and is believed to protect against nightmares.

September

Sapphire

Associated with royalty and wisdom, the Sapphire is a symbol of nobility, truth, and sincerity.

October

Opal

Opals represent hope and creativity. Each stone is a galaxy of colors, making every piece one-of-a-kind.

November

Citrine

The "Success Stone," Citrine is thought to bring energies of fortune and commercial success to the wearer.

December

Turquoise

A stone of protection and healing. Turquoise is believed to bring peace to the home and good luck to the owner.
